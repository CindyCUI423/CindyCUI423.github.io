A Bite of Qingdao

A Bite of Qingdao is a documentary recording delicacies in Qingdao.

Watch the video and you will know more about this city in terms of food, such as what the seafood market there looks like, what are the satisfying places to find delicious snacks...

Play it if you are interested

(Only Chinese subtitles available now)

Get to know the behind-the-scenes story

You probably think that shooting food-related videos must be enjoyable because you get to eat a lot of nice food along the way. But I will tell you no!

It's literally a torture to smell the food and not be able to eat it until you get a satisfying shot! Do you remember there's a big plate of seafood and a pint of beer at the end of the video? They were actually our lunch that day. We didn't start eating the dish until about two hours after it was served. We were experimenting with different camera angles, trying to find out the best way to present it.
