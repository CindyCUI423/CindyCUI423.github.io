Learn More from Nice Videos

One of the best ways to learn how to make videos is watching nice videos! Here are some videos I selected from YouTube and Vimeo for you to watch.

By clicking the button of YouTube below you can get all the feed from Looking China, where you can also find my video work. All the videos posted by Looking China are all short documentaries made by university students. Some of the students made videos for the first time, so I believe their works are of great reference.

While by clicking the button of Vimeo, you can get a list of experimental short films which I think are good for enhancing our creativity.
