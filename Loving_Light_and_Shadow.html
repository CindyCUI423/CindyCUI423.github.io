Loving Light and Shadow

Loving Light and Shadow is a documentary about shadow play, one of the most famous Chinese culture heritages.

This video tells story of Shouke Chen, the fourth generation inheritor of the Lunan Shanhua Chen’s Taoist Shadow Puppet troupe. Watch the video and you will know how he fell in love with shadow play, how to make a shadow puppet and how difficult it is to play shadow puppets well.

Play it if you are interested

Get to know the behind-the-scenes story

We went to the Zaozhuang city in July 2019 and spent 5 days there interviewing Shouke Chen and recording his shadow play performance.

Mr Chen's daily performance were prepared for the visitors of the ancient city of Tai' erzhuang, a small tourist attraction in Zaozhuang. As a result, we also got a lot of opportunities to watch the beautiful scenery of the attraction.

After finishing all the shooting, it took us about 2 weeks to edit the video. The editing of this video is not easy since the interview videos we took were too long, which means we have to spend a lot of time watching and filtering out the clips we really want.
