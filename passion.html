About Me & My Passion

This is Xinyi and welcome to my wonderland!

I am currently doing a master's degree in computing at Australian National University. One thing about me that you might find special is that I studied journalism at Ocean University of China as an undergraduate in the past 4 years. It's a huge change, isn't it?

Studying journalism has exposed me to video shooting and editing, which I later luckily found to be my passion. During the 4 years of my undergraduate study, I produced over 20 videos, including documentaries, microfilms, commercials, etc. These videos may not be masterpieces, but I had a lot of fun in the process of creating them. These are the reasons why I want to build this website—to show my video works and share the stories behind them.

About This Website / Mission Statement

This website is the best place for Xinyi's friends, family or anyone who is interested in Xinyi's video-making experiences to watch her videos and know about the stories behind them.

Visitors may find these things on this website:

✦ Some of Xinyi's video works (some videos are not currently allowed for public release) and their introduction

✦ The interesting behind-the-scenes stories of videos

✦ Xinyi's experience and tips for making videos (mainly focused on shooting and editing)

✦ Comments and questions from visitors and my replies

So this website is not just a showcase for Xinyi's personal video works, it also provides useful tips and skills to visitors on video production. Those who want to try making a video may find this site useful!
