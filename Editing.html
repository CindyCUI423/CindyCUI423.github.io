How to Edit videos

Editing can be more difficult than shooting if you have never tried it before, since you have to learn to use at least one editing software. Different editing softwares operate in different ways and you have to remember them.

Choose an Editing Software

At the very beginning, you need to select a preferred editing software. I'd like to introduce some most popular softwares for you.

Adobe Premiere Pro

My favourite editing software which is very powerful in achieving both simple and advanced effects.

It is available for both windows and apple systems.

Adobe After Effects

A very powerful editing software in making advanced video effects, but it may not user-friendly in basic editing. It is difficult to operate and not suitable for beginners in editing.

It is available for both windows and apple systems.

Final Cut Pro

A user-friendly video editing software published by Apple Inc. Similar to pr in terms of functionality.

It is only available for apple systems.

Capcut

A video editing app developed by ByteDance. It is probably the most easiest-to-use editing software with a large varieties of nice effect templates for users to use directly.

It is available for windows systems, apple systems and even mobile phones and ipad, and you can also use it online.

Editing Process

Although there is no set editing process, there is always a more efficient process to make your editing faster.

Normally, we divide the whole editing process into two stages, Rough Editing and Fine Editing.

Rough Editing

We usually do these things in the stage of rough editing.

Step1: Import all the video materials we are going to use

Step2: Cut each video materials, extract the desired clips and put then together

Step3: Add some transition effects

Step4: Add background music and adjust the clips to the music

Fine Editing

We then do these things in the stage of fine editing.

Step5: Adjust the colour, brightness,saturation and so on of our videos

Step6: Check for problems such as frame skipping

Step7: Add subtitles

Keep in mind that do not do step5 to step7 ahead of time! When you need to change or recut your video materials, all the work you have done in step5 to step7 will become meaningless. Only start fine editing when you are sure all the steps in rough editing are complete.
